About Me

Hello! I'm your local, dedicated pet caretaker/sitter. I understand that your pets are part of the family, and leaving them behind can be tough. That is why I provide personalized care that keeps their routines intact and their spirits high.


Whether it's an energetic pup needing a mid-day run or a senior cat who just wants a quiet cuddle, I offer a safe, attentive, and fun environment. I am fully insured, pet first-aid trained, and ready to provide regular photo and text updates so you have absolute peace of mind.
